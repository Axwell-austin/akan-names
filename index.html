Akan Name Generator

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born

These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and the character of the person.

Male Names

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday; Kwandwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Kwaku
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: Kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame

Female Names

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama
